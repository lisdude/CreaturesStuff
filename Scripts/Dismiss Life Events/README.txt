Dismiss Life Events v1.3
------------------------
Right-click on any life event to dismiss them all at once.
Similarly, right-click on any warp event to dismiss them all at once.

================================================================================================

Installation:
1. Copy 'Dismiss Life Events.cos' into your '\Docking Station\Bootstrap\010 Docking Station' folder.

Existing Worlds or Updating:
2. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT-C' to open the CAOS command line and type: ject "Dismiss Life Events.cos" 7
3. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT'C' again to close the command line.
