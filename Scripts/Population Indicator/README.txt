Population Indicator v1.2
-------------------------
Add indicators for creature count and egg count to the favorite places icons.

================================================================================================

Installation:
1. Copy 'Population Indicator.cos' into your '\Docking Station\Bootstrap\010 Docking Station' folder.

Existing Worlds or Updating:
2. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT-C' to open the CAOS command line and type: ject "Population Indicator.cos" 7
3. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT'C' again to close the command line.
