Ecology Monitor v1.0
-----------------------
Monitor the default ecology and reinject things as they become extinct / endangered.

================================================================================================

Installation:
1. Copy 'Ecology Monitor.cos' into your '\Docking Station\Bootstrap\010 Docking Station' folder.

Existing Worlds or Updating:
2. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT-C' to open the CAOS command line and type: ject "Ecology Monitor.cos" 7
3. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT'C' again to close the command line.
