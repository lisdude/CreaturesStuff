Routine Maintenance v1.0
------------------------
Perform routine maintenance operations every 30 minutes intended to keep the world snappy and happy.

NOTE: These tasks may not apply to you or conform to your specific preferences. Current operations are:
- Kill all Zzzz's.
- Euthanize Norns that are 1 hour old or older and still in the 'youth' life stage.
- Limit the amount of food lying around from the empathic vendor once it surpasses 100.

================================================================================================

Installation:
1. Copy 'Routine Maintenance.cos' into your '\Docking Station\Bootstrap\010 Docking Station' folder.

Existing Worlds or Updating:
2. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT-C' to open the CAOS command line and type: ject "Routine Maintenance.cos" 7
3. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT'C' again to close the command line.
