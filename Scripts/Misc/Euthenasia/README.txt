Euthenasia v1.2
------------------------
Humanely euthanize creatures that are 1 hour old or older and still in the 'youth' life stage.
Also euthanize creatures that are older than 9 hours.

================================================================================================

Installation:
1. Copy 'Euthenasia.cos' into your '\Docking Station\Bootstrap\010 Docking Station' folder.

Existing Worlds or Updating:
2. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT-C' to open the CAOS command line and type: ject "Euthenasia.cos" 7
3. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT'C' again to close the command line.
