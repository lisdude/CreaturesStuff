Rotten Eggs v1.0
------------------------
Causes eggs older than 5 hours to rot, contributing some nutrients to the soil.

If you want a timeframe different than 5 hours, you can customize the `ov00` variable in the script.

================================================================================================

Installation:
1. Copy 'Rot Eggs.cos' into your '\Docking Station\Bootstrap\010 Docking Station' folder.

Existing Worlds or Updating:
2. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT-C' to open the CAOS command line and type: ject "Rot Eggs.cos" 7
3. Press 'CTRL-SHIFT'C' again to close the command line.
